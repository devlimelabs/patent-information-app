About Edison

Simplifying patent information and democratizing access to intellectual property tools

Our Mission

Edison was created with a simple mission: to make patent information accessible, understandable, and actionable for inventors and businesses of all sizes.

Named after Thomas Edison, one of history's most prolific inventors with 1,093 US patents, our platform embodies the spirit of innovation and practical application of ideas.
